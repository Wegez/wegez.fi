PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: November 9, 2025
Data Controller: Wegez.fi

This Privacy Policy explains how Wegez.fi collects, uses, and protects the personal data of users of the website.

1. Personal Data Processed

We collect and process data only to the extent necessary for the provision of the service.

Data related to basic site usage:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, time and duration of visit.

    *Note: The website is hosted via Cloudflare Pages. Therefore, these technical data points are primarily collected by Cloudflare in their server log files for operational and security purposes. Wegez.fi's administrators do not have direct, real-time access to individual IP addresses or raw server logs; access is provided through Cloudflare's analytics/log retrieval systems.*

Data processed via the third-party (xnoverse) paywall:

Payment Data: Nano wallet address and transaction details. This data is transferred directly to and processed by the xnoverse.com service for access verification and is not stored on Wegez.fi servers.

Cookies and Local Storage

Wegez.fi itself does not use analytics or advertising cookies. However, the integrated xnoverse.com paywall may store technically necessary cookies or local storage data on the Wegez.fi domain in order to verify payment sessions and enable access control. These cookies are considered essential for the functioning of the paywall and do not involve user tracking or profiling by Wegez.fi. For details about how xnoverse handles data and cookies, please see the xnoverse Privacy Policy.

2. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

Purpose Legal Basis (GDPR)
Provision and maintenance of the service Legitimate Interest (technical functionality of the service)
Payment verification (xnoverse) Contract (granting access rights to content)
Ensuring data security Statutory Obligation and Legitimate Interest

3. Data Disclosure and Transfer

Third Parties:

For the xnoverse paywall to function, certain data (such as your wallet address) is transferred immediately to the xnoverse.com service provider to manage payments and access. Wegez.fi does not store these payment details. We recommend reviewing the xnoverse.com Privacy Policy.

Transfer of data outside the EU/EEA:

To provide the service, we may use servers or service providers located outside the European Union (EU) or the European Economic Area (EEA). In such cases, we ensure that the transfer is carried out in accordance with the GDPR.

4. Rights of the Data Subject

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  1. Right of access: You can request confirmation of whether we process personal data concerning you.
  2. Right to rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  3. Right to erasure (Right to be forgotten): You can request the deletion of your data within the limits permitted by law.
  4. Right to restriction of processing and objection: Under certain conditions, you can restrict or object to the processing of your data.
